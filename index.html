Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session? +

Choose your preferred package above and click "Book Now". You'll be taken to our secure Calendly page to select a time and complete payment. After checkout, you'll receive instant confirmation with next steps.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit/debit cards via Stripe, plus Klarna (Buy Now, Pay Later) and GoCardless (Direct Debit) for UK clients. All payments are processed on encrypted, PCI-compliant pages.

Do you offer online or in-person sessions? +

I offer both! Virtual sessions provide flexibility from anywhere, while in-person sessions (available in select locations) allow for hands-on form correction. We'll determine the best format for your needs during booking.

What if I'm a complete beginner or over 40? +

Perfect! I specialize in helping beginners and clients over 40 build confidence and foundational skills. We'll start at your current level and progress at a pace that feels challenging yet achievable. Every expert was once a beginner.

How do I access the VIP video library? +

VIP Monthly Members receive login credentials to our private coaching portal after their first payment. Videos are accessible via web or mobile app and are updated monthly with new content.
