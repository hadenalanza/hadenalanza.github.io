Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with coaching? +

It's simple! Book a free 15-minute discovery call using the calendar below. We'll discuss your goals, answer your questions, and create a personalized plan to get you started on your transformation journey.

Do you offer online or in-person sessions? +

I offer both! Virtual sessions provide flexibility and accessibility from anywhere, while in-person sessions (available in select locations) allow for hands-on form correction. We'll determine the best format for your needs during our consultation.

What if I'm a complete beginner? +

Perfect! I specialize in helping beginners build confidence and foundational skills. We'll start at your current level and progress at a pace that feels challenging yet achievable. Every expert was once a beginner.

How soon will I see results? +

While everyone's journey is unique, most clients notice improved energy and confidence within 2-3 weeks. Physical transformations typically become visible around 6-8 weeks with consistent effort. Remember: sustainable change is a marathon, not a sprint—and I'm with you every step of the way.
